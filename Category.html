EU MDR

European Union Medical Device Regulations, known as EUMDR replaces the current EU MDD (Medical Devices Directives) that regulates placing medical devices to the EU Market. All medical device manufacturers including Alcon must comply with EU MDR requirements to continue to CE mark and sell medical devices in the European Union.
